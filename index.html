Vibrant Twilight

Vibrant Twilight, often abbreviated to VT, is an online band consisting of four friends.

BACKGROUND

Aoyagi Touya, Hinomori Shizuku, and Tenma Tsukasa had been friends since childhood, however, they didn't often sing together, Tsukasa being unable to sing at all. The trio met Asahina Mafuyu in high school, who was the one who initially proposed the idea of becoming a band to Touya and Shizuku, the three inviting Tsukasa to join later on. Throughout the year, Tsukasa became more distant, and Mafuyu eventually had to move away, leaving only Touya and Shizuku.

One day, as Touya aimlessly scrolled through his phone, reminiscing about the time he had spent with the others, he found a file named "Untitled." He had initially planned to delete the file, as it hadn't been uncommon for him to find files with the same name, but his hand had slipped, and he had accidentally played the file, which had transported him to the Cottage Sekai. He had met Hatsune Miku and Kagamine Len, who had invited him for a chat.

Aoyagi Touya

LEADER / ILLUSTRATOR

“ It must be nice, having a dream to work towards. ”

Aoyagi Touya (青柳冬弥) is a senior at Kamiyama High School. He is the leader and illustrator of Vibrant Twilight, going by the aliases "Kaveh" and "Ajax".

BACKGROUND

Touya, Shizuku, and Tsukasa had been friends since childhood, though their time was limited to school. In middle school, Touya became friends with a group called Darkilight, but they caused Touya's relationship with Shizuku to become strained, especially after a conflict between Shizuku and Diluc, a former close friend of Darkilight's. Around the same time, Tsukasa began to grow more distant.

In an attempt to fix their friendship, Touya decided to push away Darkilight and invited Tsukasa out more, but his efforts were met with little success. After Darkilight faced some internal conflict and eventually disbanded, Touya attempted to reconnect with two of its former members, Mizuki and Kohane. However, their bad reputation among Touya's other friends made it difficult to connect with Touya's other friends. As a result, Touya, Shizuku, and Mafuyu invited Tsukasa to join Vibrant Twilight instead. Despite their efforts, Tsukasa remained distant, their bond further straining when Mafuyu moved away, leaving only Touya and Shizuku. Things slowly improved between them when Touya discovered the Cottage Sekai.

APPEARANCE

Touya has sharp grey eyes and a mole under the corner of his left eye. His hair is shoulder-length and bicolored, the right side a dark blue, the left a light blue, his face framed by a strand of hair on either side.

Casual Outfit

He wears a tucked black t-shirt underneath a pink-and-purple jacket and black cargo pants. Occassionally, he wears a keychain on his belt. He wears a Cinnamoroll necklace that matches with Shizuku, and a silver star necklace that matches with Shizuku and Mafuyu. He wears black-and-white sneakers. He also wears a blue bracelet with a star charm on his left wrist and a blue bracelet spelling out his name on his left wrist, both from Shizuku. He wears a blue BFF bracelet and a black and green bracelet with "BUTTERCUP" spelled out and a Buttercup charm on it, also matching with Mafuyu and Shizuku. His earrings are different, with the right one having a Bubbles charm and the left attached to a chain with a Blossom charm.

In winter, he wears a black, long-sleeved shirt under his t-shirt, with a pale yellow hoodie over the t-shirt. His jacket is still the outermost layer.

Asahina Mafuyu

LYRICIST

Asahina Mafuyu (朝比奈まふゆ) is a junior. She is the lyricist of Vibrant Twilight, going by the aliases "Luna" and "Yuki".

BACKGROUND

Throughout her childhood, Mafuyu's family frequently moved due to work. They settled for four years in one place, however, they ended up moving again to Touya's hometown. Devastated, Mafuyu withdrew emotionally, adopting a "Good Girl" persona that focused on her academic excellence. As a freshman, she tutored her peers and received straight A's, hiding her pain behind a facade of perfection.

Through a shared interest of orchestra and mutual dislike of their orchestra teacher, she eventually met Touya, Shizuku, and Tsukasa, all of whom she suggested creating a band with. Vibrant Twilight was a way for her to finally show her true self and drop the "Good Girl" persona she had kept up for many months.

However, Mafuyu's hopes of having friends and feeling better were crushed when she learned she was moving away once again. Despite keeping as much contact with Shizuku and Touya as she could, she slipped back into her "Good Girl" persona, burying her depression that gnawed at her at every waking moment.

APPEARANCE

Mafuyu has dark purple hair with eyes that fade from dark purple to sky blue in a gradient. Her hair is wavy, long, and usually kept in a braid held together by a black hair tie. Mafuyu’s bangs are fairly long with the middle section falling just a bit past her eyes. She has an ahoge that swoops to the right above her bangs, and her face is framed by short, slightly curled strands on each side.

Casual Outfit

Her casual outfit consists of a dark blue jacket. She wears a gray shirt underneath and has black leggings. She has a silver moon necklace that matches with Touya and Shizuku. She wears a purple BFF bracelet and a black and blue bracelet that spells out "BUBBLES" and a Bubbles charm on it, also matching with Touya and Shizuku.

PERSONALITY

Mafuyu's "Good Girl" persona is often shy, and she tends to act as a teacher's pet. Her voice becomes unnaturally high-pitched and forced, frequently spaceing out to conserve the energy needed to maintain this facade. When Mafuyu is around the people she truly cares about, she becomes more extroverted and energetic to the point she's unaware of what she says. When she's feeling upset while with her friends, she becomes quiet and somber, often blunt or unintentionally offensive. However, hearing her friends' voices usually helps lift her mood and re-energizes her. When Mafuyu realizes she's hurt someone she cares about, she quickly becomes apologetic and withdraws, shutting down emotionally. She often reverts to her "Good Girl" persona as a way to hide her feelings. The only person who knows how to help her in this state is Touya.

Hinomori Shizuku

VOCALIST

Hinomori Shizuku (日野森雫) is a senior at Kamiyama High School. They are a vocalist of Vibrant Twilight, going by the alias "Steph".

BACKGROUND

Shizuku became friends with Touya back in elementary school, their friendship deepening when he introduced them to Tsukasa in middle school. However, their relationship became strained after Touya got involved with a group going by the name Darkilight and their friends, leading him to become more distant. Shizuku attempted to fix their friendship, but conflicts with Darkilight, particularly an incident with one of their close friends, Diluc, made it difficult. This incident caused most of Darkilight and its outside friends, except for Touya, to despise Shizuku.

Rather than trying to connect with Darkilight again, Shizuku and Touya decided to form their own group, Vibrant Twilight, with Mafuyu, later inviting Tsukasa to join as well. Despite their efforts, Tsukasa remained distant, their bond further straining when Mafuyu moved away, leaving only Shizuku and Touya. After Darkilight eventually disbanded due to internal conflict, Shizuku saw an opportunity to reconnect with two of Darkilight's former members, Mizuki and Kohane.
